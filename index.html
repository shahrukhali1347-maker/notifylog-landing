How it works

1

Install & Grant Access

Download the APK and enable Notification Access in your device settings.

2

Capture Automatically

NotifyLog runs silently in the background, storing every notification locally.

3

Browse Your Log

Search, filter, and browse all your notifications with full-text search and date grouping.

4

Generate Insights

Tap one button to send your data to AI and get smart summaries, spending cards, and anomaly alerts.

Your data stays yours

NotifyLog stores all notification data in a local SQLite database on your device. Nothing is uploaded unless you explicitly tap "Generate Insights."
